Weight Loss Plan

Overview

This plan helps you lose weight safely by eating balanced meals and maintaining a calorie deficit.

Foods to Include

  • Vegetables, fruits
  • Whole grains
  • Lean proteins (chicken, eggs, fish)
  • Low-fat dairy
  • Nuts and seeds in small amounts

Avoid / Limit

  • Sugary drinks
  • Chips, sweets, junk food
  • Too much oil and fried food

Workout Suggestions

  • 30 min brisk walking daily
  • Strength training 2–3 days a week
  • Stretching or yoga

Tips

  • Drink 2–3 liters of water daily
  • Eat slow and mindfully
  • Sleep 7–8 hours
